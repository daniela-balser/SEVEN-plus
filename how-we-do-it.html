How we do it

 

During the entire building process of your custom e-Training experience, we will work hand in hand to create the best possible experience for you and your onboard crew.

Analysis & Structure

During the initial phase, we gather training material, identify key people, and talk to you about your training goals.

Once the analysis is completed, we break down existing training material into logical modules.

Design

In the Design phase, we create storyboards which serve as blueprints and instructions for our Graphic and Instructional Designers.

Storyboards, as well as a first draft of the course design, is sent to you for review and approval.

Development

Now, it’s time for the fun part. Imagery, graphics, video, audio, and interactive features come together and start breathing life into the project.

In the final stages of this phase, you get to test a course prototype.

Implementation

Once we receive your thumbs up for the prototype, we carry out a test phase of your
e-Training course with a designated learning group.

Following your approval, we then hand over your e-Training course to you LMS team.

Evaluation

In the next 3 to 6 months following the launch of your
e-Training course, we gather your feedback and conduct a product evaluation that measures goal achievement, performance as well as crew satisfaction.
