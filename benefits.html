HOW E-TRAINING WILL IMPACT YOUR CREW AND OPERATION

 
 
Higher Engagement through interactivity

Our customized courses feature interactive elements and challenges that will keep your employees focused and engaged during their training sessions.

Furthermore, learning content is presented visually through imagery, video and animation that aid to explain even complex workflows.

 
 
Optimized training time

According to studies, e-Training significantly reduces your training time compared to conventional face-to-face classes while providing your crew with the same knowledge levels.
How? By converting your conventional training manuals into interactive modules of approximately 5 to 20 minutes with concise content that is visually presented. An individual approach ensures crew members are focused on training relevant content thus, excluding factors which lose valuable training time.

 
 
Consistency

Unlike instructor-led-training, where content can vary with each training manager, e-Training provides identical and consistent content to each employee throughout the entire fleet.

 
 
Flexible training time

e-Training courses are built to provide flexible access to learners across various devices. Once enrolled, learners can take the course and revisit training content at any time.
This means that employees can learn in their own time as everyone has their own busy schedules, so employees can access their learning when they have time, and when their energy levels and focus are maximized for retention.

 
 
Scalable training goals

As your company grows together with your number of employees, existing training methods and development processes quickly become ineffective. e-Training liberates your company from the constraints of a busy onboard schedule by not requiring a central location and a specific time to train your personnel. Material can be accessed anywhere and anytime making your training effortless and scaled to meet demand.

 
 
World-accessibility

With e-Training your course material is available anytime and anywhere you need it. Employees can be enrolled and prepared for work on board or even before joining the ship.

 
 
Higher retention

e-Training courses contain more interactive tools than comparable in-classroom instructional sessions. Our modules allow learners to take interactive quizzes, think critically about work scenarios, and even view interactive videos. Interactive content has been proven to boost learner engagement – making e-Training an even more effective learning tool.

 
 
Cost-saving

With a one-time investment into the conversion of your training material to e-Training courses, course modules can be used as often and for as many crew members as needed, eliminating the added cost of administration and printed materials.

 
 
Reporting

e-Training courses allow in combination with a Learning Management System to enhance the performance of crew members through tracking and reporting tools. Progress of employees can be tracked and analyzed in real-time in order to identify and address areas that need improvement.
