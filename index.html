What we do

 

We at seven+ specialize in the creation and development of engaging and educational training experiences that are based on your existing hospitality training manuals.

Our creative team transforms your conventional training manuals into interactive courses that combine your service procedures, products, and standards with creativity, innovation and multimedia content. Covering various departments from Restaurant to Bar and Housekeeping, your e-Training courses give you full control of the delivered content while giving your crew the flexibility to study from home or as part of your training on board.

Contact us today or find out more about the benefits of e-Training and why our courses will make you stand out as a competitive employer to the next generation of crew members.

How we do it

 

During the entire building process of your custom e-Training experience, we will work hand in hand to create the best possible experience for you and your onboard crew.

Analysis & Structure

During the initial phase, we gather training material, identify key people, and talk to you about your training goals.

Once the analysis is completed, we break down existing training material into logical modules.

Design

In the Design phase, we create storyboards which serve as blueprints and instructions for our Graphic and Instructional Designers.

Storyboards, as well as a first draft of the course design, is sent to you for review and approval.

Development

Now, it’s time for the fun part. Imagery, graphics, video, audio, and interactive features come together and start breathing life into the project.

In the final stages of this phase, you get to test a course prototype.

Implementation

Once we receive your thumbs up for the prototype, we carry out a test phase of your
e-Training course with a designated learning group.

Following your approval, we then hand over your e-Training course to you LMS team.

Evaluation

In the next 3 to 6 months following the launch of your
e-Training course, we gather your feedback and conduct a product evaluation that measures goal achievement, performance as well as crew satisfaction.

Who we are

 

A ship is only as strong as the people sailing it...

Having worked onboard cruise ships ourselves, we are all too familiar with the challenges that your shipboard operation faces on a daily basis due to varying levels in crew knowledge.

We made it our mission to remodel the way training is delivered at sea and with this goal in mind, we started the project 'seven+' - an e-Training development agency that creates interactive training courses based on your existing hospitality manuals. As such, our courses provide flexible, performance-based learning experiences that ensure alignment of crew knowledge independent of the busy onboard operation.

 
 

Tamara Brčić

Co-Founder and Head of Instructional Design

Tamara looks back at 4 years working for a 5* cruise company where she started to form her first idea of a digitalized training course that would ensure consistency and be adaptable to the busy on board operation. With her background in digital arts and video editing, she went one step further to realize this idea and shifted her professional path towards e-learning and became a certified instructional designer.
With her creative skills and a strong eye for detail, she effortlessly converts content from in-person training modules to interactive online formats which make training not only engaging but also fun.

As part of our team, Tamara takes the lead in the instructional conversion of your e-Training course.

 
 

Daniela Balser

Co-Founder and Graphic and Web Designer

Daniela worked in the international hospitality industry, land, and sea, for more than 15 years. Whether as a certified trainer for a renown London restaurant or Beverage Manager for a 5* cruise line, Daniela's passion, and dedication for training and coaching others has always been the foundation in her career. Determined to improve and revolutionize crew training for hospitality professionals working on cruise ships, Daniela together with Tamara set the cornerstones for this project.

Within our team, Daniela focuses on the overall course structure and creates visual elements for your e-Training course.
